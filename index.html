WHO WE ARE.

A Journey of Flavor Houston to Augsburg

I’m Ali Alowa, founder of Houston Smash Burgers. Born in Saudi Arabia, raised in America, and now proud to call Augsburg home, I’ve spent years chasing the bold taste of Houston—the real, juicy, crispy-edged smash burgers you just can’t fake.

High quality standards

Our menu brings the best of American comfort food to Augsburg with flavorful pizza, crispy fries, and creamy milkshakes—crafted with care and served to the high standards our guests deserve.

Hygienic Food

fresh, never-frozen beef, smashed hot on the griddle for crispy edges and juicy centers, served on buttery brioche buns with homemade sauces and bold toppings.
